What is a Cone?

A cone is a three-dimensional geometric shape that has a circular base and a single vertex, with a curved surface connecting them. Think of an ice cream cone!

Properties

Base

1 circular face

Vertex

1 point at the top

Lateral Surface

1 curved surface

Formulas

Volume = (1/3) × π × r² × h

Surface Area = πr² + πr√(r² + h²)

where r is the radius and h is the height
