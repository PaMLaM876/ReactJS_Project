What is a Cube?

A cube is a three-dimensional solid figure with six equal square faces, twelve edges of equal length, and eight vertices.

Properties

Faces

6 square faces

Edges

12 equal edges

Vertices

8 vertices

Formulas

Volume = side³

Surface Area = 6 × side²
