Cube

A perfect three-dimensional square with equal sides and right angles throughout.

Properties:

  • 6 equal square faces
  • 12 equal edges
  • 8 vertices

Volume = side³

Surface Area = 6 × side²

Sphere

A perfectly round three-dimensional object where every point on the surface is equidistant from the center.

Properties:

  • Infinite symmetry
  • No edges or vertices
  • Perfect roundness

Volume = (4/3)πr³

Surface Area = 4πr²

Cylinder

A three-dimensional solid with circular bases connected by a curved surface.

Properties:

  • 2 circular bases
  • 1 curved surface
  • No vertices

Volume = πr²h

Surface Area = 2πr² + 2πrh

Cone

A three-dimensional shape with a circular base connected to a point by a curved surface.

Properties:

  • 1 circular base
  • 1 curved surface
  • 1 vertex

Volume = (1/3)πr²h

Surface Area = πr² + πrs
