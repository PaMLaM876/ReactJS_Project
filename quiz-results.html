Question 1: Volume of a cube

Correct Answer: side³
The volume of a cube is calculated by multiplying the side length by itself three times (cubing it).

A cylinder has two circular faces (top and bottom) connected by a curved surface.

Question 3: Sphere surface area

Correct Answer: 4πr²
The surface area of a sphere is calculated using the formula 4πr², where r is the radius.

Question 4: Shape with circular base and vertex

Correct Answer: Cone
A cone has a circular base and tapers to a single point (vertex) at the top.
